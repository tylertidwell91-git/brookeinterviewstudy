# Army National Guard Recruiter — Interview Questions

**Compiled from multiple sources** (Glassdoor blocked automated access with 403; questions below are from InterviewPrep, ResumeCat, and related recruiter/AGR board sources.)

---

## Recruiter & HR

- Can you describe your experience working with human resources processes such as recruitment, onboarding, and employee relations?
- What steps do you follow when conducting a thorough background check and security clearance for potential recruits?
- Can you provide examples of successful conflict resolution involving personnel disputes or disciplinary actions?

---

## Recruiter-Specific / AGR Board

- Questions about **POSTA** and its importance.
- **APPLE-MDT** assessment questions.
- Scenario-based: *"How would you get someone to join who wasn't interested in a bonus or college?"*
- Regulations and knowledge-based questions.

---

## Leadership & Motivation

- How have you previously demonstrated leadership qualities and the ability to motivate others towards a common goal?
- What impact do you hope to have on the National Guard during your time as a member?
- Describe a time when you had to enforce rules or regulations to maintain order and safety within a group or environment.

---

## Teamwork & Conflict

- Describe a challenging situation you faced while working in a team-oriented environment, and how you contributed to resolving it.
- How do you prioritize multiple tasks and responsibilities in high-pressure situations, especially when dealing with tight deadlines and limited resources?

---

## Standards & Performance

- How would you handle a situation where an applicant or fellow soldier is not meeting the physical fitness standards required for their role?

---

## Motivation & Values

- What inspired you to pursue a career in the National Guard?
- What do you think sets the National Guard apart from other military organizations?
- What do you think are the most important values that the National Guard upholds?
- What do you think is the most important thing that the National Guard does for its members?
- What do you think is the most important thing that the National Guard does for the country?
- What do you think is the most important thing that the National Guard does for its communities?
- What do you think is the most important thing that the National Guard does for the world?

---

## Experience & Reflection

- How has your experience in the National Guard been so far?
- What do you think of the training you've received in the National Guard?
- What do you think of the people you've met in the National Guard?
- What do you think of the places you've been in the National Guard?
- What do you think of the things you've seen in the National Guard?
- What do you think of the work you've done in the National Guard?
- What do you think of your future in the National Guard?
- What are your goals for your time in the National Guard?
- What are your plans for after your time in the National Guard?

---

## Broader Challenges & Awareness

- What do you think are the biggest challenges facing the National Guard today?
- What do you think are the biggest challenges facing the world today?
- What do you think are the biggest challenges facing our country today?

---

## General / Fit

- What are your strengths and weaknesses?
